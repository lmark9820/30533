Tao Tribe

National Science Museum

Introduction

Tao tribe is one of the sixteen tribes in Taiwan.They belong to the Austronesian family of indigenous peoples.Due to different environments, they now have different cultures, languages and living styles.

Tao boats

Tao people usually use 21 or 27 wooden to build a boat.It is difficult to find because Orchid island is small.They use special tree fibers to fill the seams to prevent water leakage.
There are three colors on Tao boat, all of which are from natural resources.White comes from seashells.Black is from coals, and red is from red dirt.
You can see three patterns on the boat.One is the human figure symbolize that their ancestors will protect the Tao. Another is the concentric circle, which is similar to the sun, serves as the eye of the boat.The Tao believe it can help them find flying fish and bless with good weather.The other is the wave totem, represented of the ocean waves.

Boat house

Boat houses, which store the Taos’ boats, are built between villages and ports.It was made by the store, and the roof is made of wooden poles covered with straws.

Tao house

Tao house has two parts.One is the main house, it is underground because of strong wind, especially during the typhoon season (summer) and monsoon (winter).Building materials are wood, stone, reeds, bamboo and thatch, all of which are local materials.
The other is a working house.There is for making tools and stoving tools. Both men and women are allowed to enter.

Flying fish

Flying fish season begins in late February or early March, ends on the 15th day of eight lunar month (usually September).After that day, the Tao people can’t eat or catch flying fish.Only dried and salted fish can be consumed during the winter.
Flying fish can be sort into three:first is best fish for women(pregnant) and children to eat.Second is second best fish for men to strength.The last is bad fish for the old.
