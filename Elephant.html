Elephant

National Science Museum

Introduction

When we enter natural science museum, we can see an elephant.It is called Penghu ancient elephant.If you take a closer look at it, you will notice that it is different from a mammoth.Penghu ancient elephant’s tusks are straight and go forward, but mammoth aren’t.
Because of the Ice age, Penghu ancient elephants migrated from Northern China to Penghu Channel.Its fossil bones were discovered there by fishermen.

圖片來源:台北市立動物園

Asian Elephant

Asian elephant’s back shape is rounded.Its weight is 5500kg.It has small and rounded ears.Only male elephants grow tusks.Its head shape is rounded and double-domed.Trunk has only one finger for grasping. It is flat teeth.Its habitat is jungle.

Mammoth

Mammoth’s back shape is rounded.Its weight is 8000kg.It’s ears are exceptionally small because it doesn’t need to let heat out.Its tusks have longer in proportion to body size more twisted and carved. And It has thicker skin and far.Its head shape is very rounded and single-domed.Trunk has only one finger for grasping. It is flat teeth.Its habitat is tundra.

圖片來源:BBC

圖片來源:台北市立動物園

African Elephant

African elephant‘s back shape is “U” shape.Its weight is 8000kg.It’s ears are longer because it need let out the heat.Both male and female have tusks.Its head shape is flatter and low-domed.Trunk has two fingers for grasping.It is diamond-shaped teeth.Its habitat is savannah and forest.
